Who we really are &
why choose us

We securitize carbon credits into fungible and tradable securities with transparent pricing and real time settlement.

Businesses and individuals alike can mitigate their unavoidable carbon emissions with the purchase of LIOH tokens, derived from high-quality, nature-based projects and verified to the highest international standards.

1 LIOH token = 1 tonne of carbon removed from the atmosphere. Buyers, consumers or wholesalers can sell or purchase LIOHs and, most importantly, retire LIOHs to offset their historic, or future carbon impact, whilst funding more carbon removal.
